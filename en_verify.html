Verification (open to anyone)

  1. Open the ENS subdomain (e.g., u-xxxx.primeid.eth).
  2. Read ENS Text: owner/controller/scope/rate/valid_until + proof (ipfs://CID) + eas:uid.
  3. Resolve EAS UID (Base/OP); fetch IPFS JSON by CID.
  4. Compare fields; if revoke=1 or expired, treat as invalid.
